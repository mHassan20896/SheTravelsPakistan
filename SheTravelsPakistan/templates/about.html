Intro

We PLAN
You Travel

She Travels Pakistan the proud pioneer of female focused travel & tours service providers to & from Pakistan, established in 2014 with a dream to make solo travelling for women possible, by empowering them and opening new doors to endless possibilities, providing them a safe comfortable and friendly environment to within Pakistan.


Recognizing the need to liberate women and setting the standards for trust, authenticity and flexibility She Travels Pakistan has expanded its services not only catering to women but to families, solo travelers, children, groups and people belonging to all age groups.


Opening windows to breathtaking landscapes, cultures and diversity of Pakistan and beyond She Travels Pakistan is the definition of evolution in modern day travelling.

Vision

We PLAN
You Travel

She Travels Pakistan is futuristic and wants to give travelers a one of kind experience by indulging their senses to take in the sight, sound and taste of nature and its bounties, making sure it is safe, secure and hits home. The core values of this female oriented travelling company are to expose local and international travelers to a hassle free and stress reliving experience that they will cherish for years to come.


It has a variety of options from planning a holiday to adventures and even volunteering and much more. A customized travelling package can be made according to the requirements of its clients, making its clients wishes always a priority we make sure that travelling alone or with peers and family is not problematic anymore.

Mission

We Bring Together People
Who Love To Travel

Our mission is to bring together people who love to travel by their common interest and passion for exploration and adventure, and take you on a trip to destinations around the globe that will take your breath away because our uniqueness lies in our diversity.


We will take you on a journey that just doesn’t focus on your pocket but allows you to meet amazing people along the way, we aim to break stereotypes and inspire the young generation and old alike to expand their horizons by realizing that travel is food for the soul.
